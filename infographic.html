Infographic Project

The infographic project was designed to help learn about the different types of communication technology. With the help of Adobe InDesign I was able to create an infographic about a compact disc. After completing research and gathering information. I was able to determine the origin, operating pinciple, uses, impacts, and possible future of a compact disc. I also learned how to find good resources and properly cite my resources.
